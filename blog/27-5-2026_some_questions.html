[blog] some questions

27 May 2026

why did you choose this research location?

in choosing a location, I knew two things: (1) I wanted to research muscles, and (2) I wanted to go to Europe (which I'd never set foot in before). my home lab PI very kindly connected me with acquaintances scattered across Europe, and the Newcastle group was the first to respond in the affirmative of being able to host me for the summer.

what assumptions are you bringing with you about your host country (e.g. country, culture, community?

a very obvious one: the food. or rather, the lack of flavor and overall enjoyment provided by the food.

also, racism toward Asians -- I've heard it's relatively prevalent across Europe.

a third assumption might be that English people are generally very reserved, stoic, and emotionally unexpressive. cool in manner, not super friendly. a little hoity-toity.

what personal strengths will help you succeed abroad?

I am pretty comfortable in my own company, which should come in handy as I explore the city's restaurants, green spaces, outdoor markets, hidden gems, and surrounding regions.
